Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Redis Database Viewer app ("we", "our", or "app") handles user data.

1. Information Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

2. Database Credentials

Users may enter Redis database connection details (such as host, port, username, and password) within the app. These credentials:

  • Are used only to establish a direct connection to the user’s database
  • Are not stored permanently by the app
  • Are not transmitted to any third-party servers

3. Data Usage

All operations are performed locally on the user's device or directly between the device and the user’s database server.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that user-provided data remains secure. However, users are responsible for securing their own database servers and credentials.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
